Name: City Kindness

----

Intro: City Kindness is a community focused partnership that serves some of the most marginalised people in Berlin.

----

Text: This partnership is made possible with the generous support of volunteers from the corporate and non-profit sectors working together to express kindness towards others. Since 2011, over 4,300 people have been impacted by these initiatives benefitting day-care centres, elderly homes, immigrant centres and community hubs.
