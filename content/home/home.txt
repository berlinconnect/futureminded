Intro: Globalisation and urbanisation are affecting everyone at some level of life, and Berlin is no exception to this. Berlin has found a way of re-emerging as a strategic urban hotspot for creativity and innovation, but with this newfound momentum our city is becoming increasingly aware of the many challenges that come with growth and change.

----

AboutHeadline: Our theory of change

----

About: We believe that the future of Berlin can be shaped positively by the resourcefulness of the people who call Berlin their home. Instead of looking either to politics or to the economy for solutions, the Futureminded Group seeks to support a collaborative innovation approach to the challenges our city faces now and in the future.
